Overview

This is the official website for the recording artist RED T7GER (redt7ger.com). It is a static, personal artist site. It currently does not run ads, does not use analytics or tracking pixels, and does not set marketing cookies. If any of this changes, this policy will be updated before those technologies are added.

What Information Is Collected

The site collects information in two places only:

  • Contact form. When you submit the form on this site, you voluntarily provide a name, email address, subject category, and message. This data is sent to Formspree, a third-party form processor, which forwards it to the RED T7GER inbox so the message can be read and replied to.
  • Server logs. The hosting provider may log standard request data (IP address, user-agent, timestamp) for abuse prevention. RED T7GER does not access or process these logs for marketing purposes.

How That Information Is Used

Contact-form submissions are used only to respond to the inquiry you sent. They are not sold, rented, or shared with any third party for marketing. They are not added to any newsletter list. They are not used to train any machine-learning model.

Third-Party Services

  • Formspree — processes contact-form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Etsy — product images on the merch section are pulled from the public RED T7GER Etsy shop (RedTigerUnlimited) for display. Clicking a product opens the listing on Etsy, which is governed by Etsy's privacy policy.
  • Streaming and social platforms — links to Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, SoundCloud, and X open in a new tab. Once you leave this site, those platforms' privacy policies apply.

Analytics and Tracking

This site currently uses no analytics software, tracking pixels, or advertising attribution tools.

In the future, privacy-respecting analytics (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics or Google Analytics) may be added to understand organic search traffic and site performance. If that happens, this policy will be updated to name the tool, describe what is collected, and explain how to opt out.

If advertising attribution pixels (such as the Meta Pixel) are ever added for campaigns that direct traffic to this site, this policy will be updated to describe exactly what is tracked, for what purpose, and how you can opt out or request deletion of any data collected. RED T7GER's current ad campaigns send traffic directly to streaming platforms and external stores — not to this site — so no such pixel is in use today.

Cookies

This site currently does not set any cookies. If analytics or advertising attribution tools are added in the future (see Analytics and Tracking above), a notice will be displayed and this section will be updated to describe the cookies used, their purpose, and duration.
